About grandvw.io

grandvw.io is a smart video analytics platform designed to help teams monitor, track, and evaluate operations in real-time. Whether you’re managing safety protocols, tracking equipment, or automating alerts, our tools empower you to stay informed and act faster.

🎯 Mission

To simplify real-time video analysis and deliver actionable insights across industries through a user-friendly, scalable platform.

⚙️ How It Works

We use AI-powered object detection and customizable dashboards to convert video streams into structured data — complete with alerts, summaries, and visualizations.

🚀 Built For

Operators, safety teams, and technical managers looking to improve visibility, accountability, and automation in dynamic environments.
